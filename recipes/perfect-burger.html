The Perfect Burger

Description

I was skeptical at first but this might be one of the most perfect burgers.

I think a big part is the portuguese sesame bun and the special sauce!

Ingredients

  • USDA Prime Angus
  • Slivered Onion
  • X-Special Sauce
  • Marinated Tomato
  • Double Sharp Cheddar
  • Shaved Iceberg
  • Habanero Pickle
  • Portuguese Sesame Bun

Steps

  1. Cook patty to perfection
  2. Place livered onion on bottom bun
  3. Place pickles on top of onions
  4. Place iceberg on top of pickles
  5. Place patty on top of iceberg
  6. Place sauce and rest of the dope ingredients on top
