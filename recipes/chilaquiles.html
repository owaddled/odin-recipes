Chilaquiles

Description

Chilaquiles are one of man's greatest inventions.

They have the crispiness of tortilla chips, plus a lil mush with the salsa interface

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Salsa
  • Egg
  • Meat (optional)
  • Love

Steps

  1. Fry the tortillas
  2. Drain the tortilla chips
  3. Coat the chips with salsa
  4. Cook an egg and add to chips
  5. Serve Warm
