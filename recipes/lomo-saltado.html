Lomo Saltado

Description

The only food better than chilaquiles. Lomo Saltado

The french thought they perfected carbs+meat with pomme frites but Lomo Saltado adds soy sauce and the optional fried egg (montado style)

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Red Onions
  • Scalions
  • Tomato
  • Rice
  • French Fries

Steps

  1. Cook steak in the pan
  2. Stir fry the veggies
  3. Add beef back to the pan with the veggies
  4. Add to plate with french fries and rice
